# Assessment Impacted Modules

| Module / Capability | Impact |
|---|---|
| Wellness Centre (VRC) | Assessment is part of the VRC customer journey and treatment planning |
| Booking Management | Booking may depend on completed assessment where mandatory |
| Package Management | Recommendation may influence package selection |
| Treatment Management | Treatment planning depends on assessment outcome |
| Reporting | Assessment outcomes and recommendations may be reported |
| Customer Portal | Future display of assessment status or recommendation may be considered |
| Staff Portal | Staff may create, conduct and complete assessment records |

## Impact Notes
Assessment is a core capability for VRC but may have different applicability in Resident or other modules.

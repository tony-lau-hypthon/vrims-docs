# Assessment Management Capability Summary

## Business Objective
Assessment Management ensures that customer treatment planning is based on an appropriate assessment process where required.

## Core Responsibilities
- Create assessment record.
- Assign suitable therapist or professional.
- Conduct assessment.
- Record assessment findings.
- Complete assessment.
- Generate treatment recommendation.
- Support downstream booking and package planning.

## Relationship with Booking
Booking uses Assessment Management to determine whether treatment booking can proceed.

If assessment is mandatory but not completed, booking confirmation should not proceed until the assessment requirement is resolved.

## Relationship with Package
Package Management may use assessment recommendation to support package selection or package purchase planning.

## Relationship with Reporting
Assessment outcomes may be used for management reporting, treatment recommendation review, and operational follow-up.

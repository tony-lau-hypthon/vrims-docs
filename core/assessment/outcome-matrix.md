# Assessment Outcome Matrix

| Outcome | Business Meaning | Next Action |
|---|---|---|
| Fit for treatment | Client can proceed to recommended treatment | Proceed to treatment recommendation and booking |
| Need further assessment | More information is required | Schedule follow-up assessment |
| Not suitable for selected treatment | Requested treatment is not recommended | Recommend alternative treatment or stop booking |
| Refer to external / other service | VRC treatment not suitable at this stage | Manual follow-up by staff |

## Notes
This matrix is business-level only. Clinical scoring, detailed assessment form design and medical judgement rules are out of scope for Review Version.

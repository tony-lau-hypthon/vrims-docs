# Billing Management Capability Summary Review

## Business Objective
Ensure billable business events are converted into auditable finance records, invoices, payments and adjustments.

## Core Responsibilities
- Create charges from billable events.
- Generate invoices.
- Track payment status.
- Allocate payments.
- Handle outstanding balance.
- Process credit notes.
- Process refunds.
- Handle write-offs.
- Support referral fee settlement.
- Maintain billing audit history.

## Relationship with Other Capabilities
- Booking may trigger billable events.
- Package determines whether customer is charged by redemption or single-session billing.
- Notification may send invoice generated and payment reminder events.
- Reporting uses billing records for finance reports.

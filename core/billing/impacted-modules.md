# Billing Impacted Modules

| Module / Capability | Impact |
|---|---|
| Wellness Centre (VRC) | Assessment charge, treatment charge, package purchase, invoice and payment |
| Booking Management | Completed treatment may trigger charge or package-related billing |
| Package Management | Package purchase and redemption affect billing / finance handling |
| Notification | Invoice generated and payment reminder events |
| Reporting | Revenue, outstanding balance, credit note, refund and referral fee reports |
| Finance | Payment allocation, credit note, refund, write-off |
| Customer Portal | Future invoice/payment visibility |
| Staff Portal | Staff may view charge/payment status depending on role |

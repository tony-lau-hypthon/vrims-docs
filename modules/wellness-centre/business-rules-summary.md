# Wellness Centre Business Rules Summary

## Booking
- Booking requires staff confirmation.
- Customer cannot choose therapist.
- Customer cannot cancel or reschedule online.
- Therapist and room cannot be double-booked.
- No-show does not deduct package.

## Package
- Package must be validated before confirmation where applicable.
- Earliest-expiry package is consumed first.
- Redemption occurs after completed treatment.
- Package transaction history must be retained.

## Assessment
- Assessment may be mandatory before selected treatments.
- Completed assessment may generate treatment recommendation.
- Treatment booking should align with assessment recommendation where mandatory.

## Notification
- Notification is triggered by business events.
- Notification templates are centrally managed.

## Billing
- Billing is triggered by billable business events.
- Invoice must have identifiable owner.
- Credit note must reference original invoice.
- Billing history must be auditable.

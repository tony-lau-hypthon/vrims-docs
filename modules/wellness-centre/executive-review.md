# Wellness Centre Executive Review

## Summary
The Wellness Centre module supports the VRC business process from enquiry to treatment completion and downstream package/billing handling.

## Key Confirmed Direction
- Booking is staff-controlled.
- Customer cannot select therapist.
- Customer cannot cancel/reschedule online.
- Assessment may be required before treatment.
- Package validation happens before booking confirmation where applicable.
- Package redemption happens after completed treatment.
- No-show does not deduct package entitlement.
- Billing and credit note handling require auditability.

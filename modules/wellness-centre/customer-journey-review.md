# Wellness Centre Customer Journey

## Main Journey

```text
Enquiry
  ↓
Registration
  ↓
Assessment
  ↓
Treatment Recommendation
  ↓
Package Purchase / Payment Arrangement
  ↓
Booking Request
  ↓
Reception Review
  ↓
Booking Confirmation
  ↓
Treatment
  ↓
Package Redemption / Billing
  ↓
Follow-up / Reporting
```

## Business Understanding

The Wellness Centre journey is service-oriented. Booking is one part of the customer journey, but the complete VRC process also includes assessment, package entitlement, treatment completion and billing.

## Review Notes

- Assessment is confirmed as mandatory before treatment types, and may sometimes be free of charge.
- Booking behaviour should follow `VRIMS-BKG-001`.
- Package redemption should occur after treatment completion, not at booking request stage.

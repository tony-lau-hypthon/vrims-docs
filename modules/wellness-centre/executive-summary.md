# Executive Summary

The Wellness Centre (VRC) module supports VRC client operations from registration and assessment through booking, treatment, package handling, billing and reporting.

The first review version focuses on confirming the business operating model rather than Odoo configuration or technical implementation.

## Key Business Principles

- VRC operates as a rehabilitation / wellness service module.
- VRC booking is staff-controlled.
- Customers can request bookings but cannot choose therapist.
- Booking confirmation is handled by VRC staff.
- Package validation and redemption are connected to booking and treatment completion.
- Assessment may be mandatory before treatment and may sometimes be free of charge.
- Promotions and member discounts cannot be combined.
- Reports are required for operational and management review.

## Current Readiness
The Wellness Centre module is considered ready for Business Review, with Booking Management already consolidated as a reusable core capability.

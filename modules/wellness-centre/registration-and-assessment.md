# Registration

The VRC client record includes:

- personal identity
- contact information
- date of birth
- identification information
- address
- emergency contact
- insurance indication
- enquiry / marketing source
- PICS acknowledgement
- direct-marketing consent

PICS acknowledgement and direct-marketing consent are separate controls.

# Assessment

## Current Rule

**Knowledge Status:** Validated  
**Source Type:** Business Clarification  
**Validation Status:** Not Required

Assessment is required before purchasing any VRC package.

## Functional Design Required

Define an assessment matrix covering:

- required assessment type
- applicable service or package
- validity period
- reassessment trigger
- whether the assessment is chargeable
- approval authority for a free assessment

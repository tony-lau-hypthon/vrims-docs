# Decision Standard

## Decision ID Format

```text
D-NNNN
```

Example:

```text
D-0001
```

## Decision Template

```markdown
## D-0001 — Decision Title

**Status:** Confirmed / Superseded / Pending  
**Date:**  
**Decision Owner:**  
**Source:**  

### Decision

Decision statement.

### Rationale

Why this decision was made.

### Impact

Impacted capabilities and modules.

### Supersedes / Superseded By

Historical tracking.
```

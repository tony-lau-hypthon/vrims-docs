# AI Authoring Guide

This guide is for AI assistants maintaining VRIMS documentation.

## Rules

1. Never duplicate business rules across documents.
2. Reference core capability documents instead of copying them.
3. Do not silently overwrite previous decisions.
4. Mark uncertain items as TBD or Assumption.
5. Every confirmed rule must have evidence.
6. Separate confirmed facts, assumptions, conflicts, open questions, decisions and related modules.
7. Use standard IDs for rules, scenarios, decisions and acceptance criteria.
8. Maintain traceability from evidence to acceptance criteria.
9. Do not write technical implementation unless explicitly requested.
10. Markdown is the source of truth; Word/PDF are outputs.

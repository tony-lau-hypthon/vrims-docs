# Evidence Priority Standard

Priority (highest to lowest)

1. Decision Evidence
2. Business Confirmation
3. Tony Confirmation
4. Approved Master Data
5. Approved Functional Design
6. Raw Requirements
7. Supporting Evidence
8. Model Inference

Higher-priority evidence prevails. Conflicts must be documented, never silently overwritten.

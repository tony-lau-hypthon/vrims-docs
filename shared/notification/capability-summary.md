# Notification Capability Summary

## Purpose
Provide a reusable notification capability shared by Booking, Assessment, Package, Billing and future modules.

## Supported Channels
- Email
- SMS
- WhatsApp (future)
- Push Notification for Customer Portal PWA
- In-app Notification

## Audience
- Customer
- Resident
- Family
- Therapist
- Reception
- Finance
- Management


## Portal Scope Clarification

The Customer Portal web application with PWA capability is current scope for VR Resident, VRC Member and VC Member. Detailed push-notification implementation remains subject to portal functional design.

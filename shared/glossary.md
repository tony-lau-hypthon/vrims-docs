# Shared Glossary

## VRC Client

A client receiving services from Ventria Rehabilitation Centre.

## VR Resident

A resident of Ventria Residence.

## VC Member

A Ventria Club member.

## Booking

A scheduled appointment or service reservation.

## Treatment

A therapy or rehabilitation service delivered to a client.

## Package

A prepaid entitlement for a set of services or sessions.

## Assessment

Clinical or service assessment performed before treatment.

## Reception

Staff role responsible for client handling, booking confirmation and operational coordination.

## Therapist

Professional delivering treatment or assessment.

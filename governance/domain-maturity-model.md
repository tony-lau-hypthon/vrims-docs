# Domain Maturity Model

## Purpose

Provide a consistent maturity standard for every business domain in the repository.

## Levels

### Level 0 — Unclassified
- Evidence exists but has not been inventoried.
- No reliable domain model.
- Not ready for repository update.

### Level 1 — Foundation
- Core evidence inventoried.
- Main entities, workflows, terms, and business rules identified.
- Open questions and conflicts recorded.
- Supporting evidence may remain deferred.

### Level 2 — Extended
- Supporting evidence analysed.
- Reports, samples, forms, layouts, and operational examples reviewed.
- Coverage gaps reduced.

### Level 3 — Verified
- Tony review completed.
- Knowledge classifications confirmed.
- Major business rules and assumptions explicitly separated.
- Domain is ready for controlled output generation.

### Level 4 — Governed
- Knowledge integrity regression completed.
- Traceability is sufficient.
- Conflicts and duplicates are resolved or explicitly recorded.
- Domain is accepted as canonical current state.

### Level 5 — Operationally Maintained
- Delta ingestion process is active.
- Freshness and supersession are monitored.
- Functional design and implementation references remain linked.

## Completion Criteria

A domain cannot be promoted by percentage alone. Promotion requires evidence, review, classification, traceability, and integrity checks.
